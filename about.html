About us

Founded in 2019,
Talanta.ke is a national nonprofit that uses a performance based approach to help tens of thousands of inner-city youth and their families create success in their lives.

Our Vision Our Mission Impacts
We transform the lives of youth and poor communities using the developmental power of performance,

a new way of engaging poverty.		 Using the power of performance,
we help youth see new possibilities for themselves and their communities.
